Talisman of the Animal Spirit

While wearing this Talisman you are able to use Wild shape once more than your druid level allows you per long rest.
While in Wild shape, any attack made against an enemy counts as being from a magical weapon when determining resistances and immunities. Each attack also gains an extra 1d6 worth of damage of the same type as the attack itself.
During a long rest you can choose one of the spells you prepare for the next day. You can now cast that spell while in Wild shape.
